What Mozaiq is

Mozaiq is a daily color logic puzzle for iPhone. Every board is a small mosaic split into sections — connected runs of cells that all share one color. Your job is to figure out which section gets which paint, using the colored hint cells as anchors.

The twist: when two sections cross, the cell at their intersection shows the mixed color of the two — yellow plus blue makes green, red plus yellow makes orange, red plus blue makes purple. Read the mix, work backwards to the section colors, fill the board, beat the clock.

Four difficulty tiers, a fresh puzzle every day, and a global leaderboard that rewards fewer moves and faster solves.

How to play.

Three colors. One paintbrush. A timer ticking down.

  1. Pick a paint — yellow, blue, or red, from the row at the bottom.
  2. Tap a section to fill every cell in it with that color.
  3. Watch the intersections auto-mix. They turn orange, green, or purple based on the two sections that cross.
  4. Match the puzzle by giving every section the right color before time runs out.

How to win.

Read the hints. Trust the mix. Don't guess.

  • Every solvable puzzle has one unique answer — no guessing required, ever.
  • Colored hint cells lock in known colors. Mixed-color hints constrain two sections at once — the math always works out.
  • Solve in par moves for the full three-star rating. Solve fast for leaderboard glory.
  • Stuck? Hit Reset and try a different read of the hints. The clock keeps going.
