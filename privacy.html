Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Mozaiq ("we", "us") collects when you use the Mozaiq iOS app (the "App"), how we use it, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible and never sell or share your data for advertising.

1. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address — required to create and sign in to your account.
  • Password — stored only as a one-way salted hash by our authentication provider (Supabase). We cannot read your password.

Profile (optional, leaderboard-only)

  • Username — only created if you choose to claim a spot on the public leaderboard. Visible to other players. You can pick a name unrelated to your real identity.

Gameplay data

  • Scores, move counts, elapsed time, stars, completion dates — per puzzle, tied to your account. Used to show your personal "Best runs" and to power the leaderboard if you choose to participate.

On-device storage

  • Authentication tokens — stored locally on your device by the operating system so you don't have to sign in every time. Removed when you sign out.
  • Tutorial seen flag — a small marker so we don't show the tutorial twice.

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that profile your behavior.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, location, calendar, or health data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in across app launches.
  • To save and display your puzzle scores and progress.
  • To show the global leaderboard, including your username and score if you've chosen to submit.
  • To send transactional emails: account confirmation, password reset.

4. Who we share data with

We don't sell, rent, or trade your data. We use the following service providers to run Mozaiq, each bound by their own privacy commitments:

  • Supabase — hosts our authentication and database. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple — distributes the app via the App Store and may collect standard app installation diagnostics under their own policy.

We may disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect users' safety or our legal rights.

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from inside the App. When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your authentication record (email, password hash)
  • Your profile (username)
  • All your scores and gameplay history
  • Your spot on any leaderboards

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

6. Children's privacy

Mozaiq is rated for ages 4+ but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please email us at bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com and we will delete it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct — ask us to update inaccurate data (you can also edit your username in the App).
  • Delete — delete your account and all associated data from inside the App, or request deletion by email.
  • Object — to certain uses of your data.

EU/UK users have additional rights under GDPR. California residents have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. To exercise any of these rights, email bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are stored as one-way salted hashes by our authentication provider. Access to the database is gated by row-level security policies so users can only read and modify their own data, except for opted-in leaderboard usernames and scores which are visible to other authenticated users.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com.
