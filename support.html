Common questions

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email. We'll send you a link to reset it.

How do I delete my account?

Open the App → Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, scores, and any leaderboard spots. It cannot be undone.

Why didn't my score show up on the leaderboard?

Leaderboards only include scores you've opted to submit. After a qualifying run, you'll be prompted to claim your spot. You can enable "Always submit my scores" in that prompt to skip the confirmation for future runs.

I want to report an offensive username

Email us at bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com with the username and what puzzle's leaderboard you saw it on. We review reports within 48 hours and remove content that violates our Terms of Service.

I have feedback or a feature request

We'd love to hear it. Email us at bostondevelopmentco@gmail.com.
